Get Help Fast

Welcome! This page covers setup, common issues, and how to contact us. If you're new, start with Getting Started. For billing and subscriptions, see Contractor Pro.

Getting Started

  • Open the app and sign in.
  • Go to Menu → VIP Center to view the paywall and upgrade to Contractor Pro Monthly.
  • Explore the Live Map, Weather Radar, and Supply tools from the home screen.

Key Features

  • Live Weather Radar — visualize storms and conditions for your job sites.
  • Smart Maps — real‑time pins, reporting, and contractor locations.
  • Supply Management — dashboards and analytics to track resources.
  • Contractor Pro — premium tools unlocked with a monthly subscription.

Billing & Subscriptions

Contractor Pro Monthly is an auto‑renewable subscription available via in‑app purchase.

  • Manage or cancel anytime in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device.
  • Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before renewal.
  • Purchases are tied to your Apple ID; restoring is available via Restore Purchases in the paywall.

Troubleshooting

  • Products not loading: Ensure you’re signed in with your Apple ID, have network access, and try again.
  • Offerings empty: If you’re a tester, sign in with a Sandbox account on your device.
  • Restore missing: Tap Restore Purchases and make sure you’re using the same Apple ID.
  • Location/Map issues: Check device permissions for Location and Network.
