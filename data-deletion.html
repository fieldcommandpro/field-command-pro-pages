Account & Data Deletion — Field Command Pro

How to permanently delete your account and personal data associated with Field Command Pro.
Effective date: August 13, 2025
Up to 30 daysfor deletion to complete
3 methodschoose any option below
No 2FA requiredfor reviewer/test accounts

Option A In-App Deletion (Recommended)

  1. Open the app and sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy → Delete Account.
  3. Review the notice and confirm. Your account and personal data will be deleted or anonymized within 30 days, unless we’re legally required to retain some records.

Option B Email Request

Send us an email and we’ll process deletion for you.

  1. Email Info@FieldCommandPro.com with subject “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include the email/username used for your account and a brief confirmation that you wish to permanently delete it.
  3. We’ll verify ownership and complete the request within 30 days.

Option C Install, Log In & Delete

If you’re not currently signed in on a device:

  1. Install Field Command Pro from the app store.
  2. Log in using the account you want to delete.
  3. Navigate to Settings → Delete Account and confirm.

This is the same as Option A, but spelled out for users who need to reinstall first.

What gets deleted?

  • Identity data (name, email/username, profile ID)
  • Profile & preferences
  • In-app content such as pins, comments, alerts
  • Subscription entitlements tied to the account
  • General app activity associated with your profile

We may retain limited records when necessary to comply with law, resolve disputes, prevent fraud/abuse, or meet accounting obligations (e.g., payment/transaction logs). These are held only for as long as required.

Need help?

If you encounter any issues deleting your account, contact our support team at Info@FieldCommandPro.com. Please write from the email associated with your account so we can verify ownership quickly.
