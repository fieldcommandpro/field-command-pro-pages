How to Delete Your Data

Effective date: August 13, 2025

Option A: In‑App Request (Recommended)

Open the App and go to [Settings > Privacy > Delete account/data]. Confirm the request. We will delete or anonymize your personal data associated with Facebook Login within 30 days, unless we must keep it for legal reasons.

Option B: Email Request

Send an email to Info@FieldCommandPro.com with the subject “Data Deletion Request” and include the Facebook account email you used with the App. We will verify your identity and process the request within 30 days.

What data is deleted?

We delete or anonymize personal data linked to your account (e.g., name, email, profile ID, and App activity). Some records may be retained if required by law, to prevent fraud/abuse, or for security.

Questions?

Contact: Info@FieldCommandPro.com

Note for developers: You can point Meta’s “User Data Deletion” field to this page or implement an automated deletion callback endpoint.
