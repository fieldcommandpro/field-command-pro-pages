How it works

  1. Tap Report Snowfall in the app.
  2. We use your device’s precise GPS (with your permission) to capture latitude & longitude.
  3. Your report becomes a pin on the Field Command Pro map for everyone to see.

Sign‑in options include username & password, plus OAuth (Google and Facebook). Account deletion is available in‑app at Settings → Delete Account.
